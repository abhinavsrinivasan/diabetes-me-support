‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Open the app and tap "Sign up" on the welcome screen. Enter your email, create a password, and verify your email address to get started.

How do I track my daily nutrition?

Go to your Profile tab and use the Daily Goals section. You can manually add nutrition values or add them directly from recipes by tapping the "+" button on recipe cards.

How does the barcode scanner work?

Tap the Scanner tab and point your camera at any food product barcode. The app will analyze the nutrition information and provide a diabetes-friendliness rating.

Can I save my favorite recipes?

Yes! Tap the heart icon on any recipe to add it to your favorites. View all saved recipes in the Favorites tab in your Profile.

How do I reset my daily progress?

Your daily progress automatically resets at midnight. You can also manually reset it by tapping the refresh icon in the Daily Goals section.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and secure authentication. Your personal health data is stored securely and never shared with third parties.

How do I delete my account?

Currently, account deletion must be requested through our support email. We're working on adding this feature directly to the app.

App crashes or won't load

Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If the problem persists, restart your device or reinstall the app.

Login issues

Make sure you're using the correct email and password. Check that your email has been verified. If you forgot your password, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen.

Barcode scanner not working

Ensure the app has camera permissions enabled in your device settings. Clean your camera lens and ensure good lighting when scanning.

Recipes not loading

Check your internet connection. The app requires an active internet connection to load recipe data.
